Our Services

Everything students need to succeed in Japan.

From Japanese language fluency to university admission support, Majime provides the complete roadmap for academic success in Japan.

Japanese Language Classes

Learn Japanese with experienced instructors through structured, practical, results-oriented classes tailored for Japan-bound students from Nepal.

JLPT Preparation

Comprehensive preparation for the Japanese Language Proficiency Test (JLPT) at all levels — N5 through N1 — with expert coaching and practice tests.

NAT Preparation

Targeted preparation for the Nihongo Achievement Test (NAT) — a key language benchmark for students aspiring to study at Japanese language schools.

JLCT Preparation

Specialized training for the Japanese Language Certification Test (JLCT) to strengthen language credentials needed for Japan university admission.

JPT Preparation

Focused preparation for the Japanese Proficiency Test (JPT), helping students achieve scores required for university admission and scholarship applications.

J-CERT Preparation

Expert preparation for the J-CERT proficiency certification, equipping students with the recognition required for admission to Japanese institutions.

One-on-One Career Counseling

Personalized counseling sessions helping each student choose the right academic path, institution, and career direction aligned with their individual goals.

University Admission Support

End-to-end assistance with Japan university and language school applications — from school selection and interview preparation to enrollment confirmation.

Documentation Support

Flawless documentation preparation ensuring complete, accurate, and visa-ready paperwork — the backbone of our 95%+ visa success rate.
