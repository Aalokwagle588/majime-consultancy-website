About Majime International Education

Built to support students beyond admission.

Majime International Education Pvt. Ltd. was founded with one clear mission — to satisfy students through honest guidance, strong preparation, and a lifetime support system that continues throughout their journey in Japan.

Our Story

A vision started in 2018 A.D.

In 2018, co-founders Kishor Subedi and Basanta Parajuli set out with a unique vision: to create a support system for students in Japan that lasts a lifetime.

Their promise to parents and students goes beyond admission letters. Majime International Education stays by its students' sides throughout their journey abroad, helping them overcome hurdles and learn the ropes.

By sharing real-life experiences with new students, the consultancy ensures that every student who follows in their footsteps enjoys a smoother, easier, and more successful life in Japan.

Our Main Goal

To Satisfy the Students.

We believe the right counseling, the right documentation, and the right preparation can change a student's future. Every decision we make centers on one goal — student satisfaction and lifelong success.

Established 2018 A.D. • Khairahani, Chitwan, Nepal
